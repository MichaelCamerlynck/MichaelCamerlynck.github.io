A bit about myself

So who am I?

My name is Michael Camerlynck and I am an 18 yo student attending the applied computer science class at Thomas More. I am making this website as a portfolio and a platform to show what I have learned for the first semester of webdesign essentials.

Bali

I spent most of my early life in Indonesia, and the most memorable place for me there is Bali. I moved to Bali when I was 15 and I enjoyed my time there greatly. Bali is a small island east of Java that is filled with beautiful places and rich culture and traditions. If you're ever going there and would like some recommendation on where to go, feel free to contact me.

Hobbies

Something to know about me is that there's always earphones in my ears. I am constantly listening to something either music or podcasts. I listen to a many genres of music such as pop, lo-fi, indie, old band, and music with genres that I have no idea how to categorize.

As for my free time, I usually spend it playing games or reading a good book. I enjoy competitive fps games and also some rich story driven games with interesting world building, in fact I like them so much I made an entirly sepate page here where you can get a top 5 list.
